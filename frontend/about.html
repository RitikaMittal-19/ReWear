ReWear was born from a simple belief: great style shouldn't cost the earth. We're building the most trusted community for pre-loved fashion in India.

Why ReWear?

The fashion industry is one of the world's largest polluters. Every year, millions of garments end up in landfills while perfectly wearable clothes sit unused in wardrobes.

ReWear creates a circular economy for clothing. When you list an item, you earn points. When you claim an item, you spend points. No money changes hands — just a community sharing what they love.

Every exchange on ReWear saves water, reduces carbon emissions, and keeps clothing out of landfills. Together, our community has already prevented over 100 tonnes of textile waste.
